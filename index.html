About the Event

The Sambus Geospatial Summit 2026 is the premier gathering for GIS professionals, remote sensing experts, surveyors, and UAV operators across West and East Africa. Over three days, more than 450 attendees will experience hands-on workshops, keynote addresses, live technology demonstrations, and networking opportunities with the continent's leading geospatial practitioners.

Hosted at the Transcorp Hilton Abuja, SGS2026 showcases Sambus's full product portfolio — Esri ArcGIS, ENVI, Wingtra, and Trimble — through structured sessions, field demonstrations, and exhibition floor interactions.
